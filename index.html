IMPACT OF MPPT

Let's discuss the impacts of MPPT as follows

After completing the steps above, Run withoutMPPT.slx and observe second window of scope the peak first reach to the maximum output of wind turbine then it fluctuates due to changes in
  • Wind
  • Pitch

Then open MPPT_GO and observe second window again now there is a shoot due to startup conditions and after very few time the output gets stable at its max value that is 4000-5000 watt.
